# PROPOSAL.md

## Client Brief

**Client:** Mudita Studios — a venture studio that supports early-stage portfolio companies with go-to-market strategy, growth operations, and AI-native tooling.

**Purpose:** Design and prototype an AI-powered micro-SaaS application that solves a real friction point in early-stage go-to-market (GTM) workflows for startup teams.

**Audience:** Early-stage startup founders, small marketing teams, and growth operators who deal with recurring GTM bottlenecks — messy lead targeting, low-conversion outbound, unclear ad ROI, fragmented data across tools, and reactive campaign iteration. They need focused, AI-native tools that unlock efficiency without requiring a full platform or dedicated ops hire.

**Key action:** Identify a specific GTM friction point, build a working micro-SaaS prototype that addresses it, and present a live or recorded demo showing the user journey from input to output.

**Pages/sections needed:**
- Product overview and personal background (problem, audience, solution, and why it matters)
- Live demo or recorded walkthrough of the prototype (3–5 minutes)
- Architecture snapshot (stack, APIs, models, data flow)
- GTM rationale (how the tool fits into a real workflow and drives a business outcome)
- Future vision (scaling, integrations, pricing, and expansion post-MVP)

**Content status:** The problem space and evaluation criteria are defined by the Mudita case brief. The specific friction point, product concept, prototype, presentation deck, and demo video all need to be developed. Sample data for the demo will need to be created or sourced independently.

**Style preferences:** No specific visual direction provided by the client. The deliverable is a presentation plus a working demo, so the prototype should prioritize clarity and usability over visual polish. Clean, functional, and professional — the tool should feel like something a founder would actually use.

**Inspiration sites:**
- Mudita's sample concepts (LeadLoom, AdPulse, SyncNote, MarketMap) — each demonstrates a focused, single-feature product with a clear value proposition tied to a specific GTM bottleneck
- AI-assisted build tools like Cursor, Lovable, Replit, and v0 — referenced as recommended development platforms for rapid prototyping
